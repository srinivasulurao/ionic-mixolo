Who can host a Mixolo event?

Any person or organization can host an event, provided they are legally operating entity. Sole proprietorships may host an event if they are registered with their home state and/ or have a verifiable digital footprint (website, mobile app, social media, online reviews...).

Mixolo wants to democratize event planning and hosting, increate the number of opportunities for people to connect in person, with or withour a plus-one, and encourage establishtments to open their doors and minds to a new creative (and fun) level of customer engatement of our groups of solos.
